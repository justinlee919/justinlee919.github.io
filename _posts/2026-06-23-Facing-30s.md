It is still surreal to me for getting the "Case Approved" notification in my USCIS account. I woke up on a random sunday morning,
and then once I opened the email. I couldn't believe my eyes, I was free. Free at last, from the never ending immigration torture,
for worrying about finding a job in 90 days if i ever got laid off, from the stress of being constrained to a company or a place.

I was free, free as a bird. I remember 6 years ago, landing in sea-tac airport, the chilly November winds of Seattle, if I close my eyes, it could be San Francisco's chilly foggy streets and neighborhoods. Everything has changed, and yet compare to 6 years ago, I am more unhappy than ever. 
For the first time in a long time, I am free and lost and anxious all at the same time. 

You ever realize how far you've been pushed forward in life, and then you realize you don't even know where you stand? That's me right now.
